Our Latest Works

We have started putting pen to paper some 5 years ago and have since stroke up a tight friendship, which in turn has yielded a great deal of professional satisfaction. As linguistic specialists, we work very much at ease with each other and occasionally start our day by serving morning appetizers on a good slice of steamy bread and some healthy giggles. We work in mainstream languages but go off-stream in our approach to quality work. We do our work scrupulously, with excellence, and we make painstaking efforts to make good on our every engagement. All while keeping up the humor!

We are certified to competently take on linguistic projects and, in exchange, we have made a vow to deliver on our formation and expertise. Throughout our development years we have tackled various translation fields and eventually chose to branch out into subdomains, thus polishing up and perfecting our development in directions such as legal, financial, banking and IT.

We are idealists and like to crisscross misconceptions and monetary barriers by also supporting national volunteer translation initiatives and promoting a healthy way of practicing our profession. And we are not only linguists by formation, but have also taken up teaching, project management and administration roles that blend well with our quiet family life and shape our development as individuals.
