Our Process

By and large, here is how we do it!

A project well begun is half done. We give each of our tasks due consideration and time so that your business objective is met. By and large, here is how we do it:

  1. once we receive your request for linguistic services, we analyse the business need, evaluate the project implications and assess its technical and regulatory components;
  2. next, we develop a high-level milestone view of the project and discuss the standard planning process by collecting and verifying the source files, reference materials and translation kit;
  3. then, we create a dedicated translation memory and opt in for the dedicated expert linguist(s)/subject-matter expert(s) by factoring in the time sensitiveness of your task;
  4. once that is done we kick off your project and attentively coordinate the communication and content flow in-between team members; in the meanwhile, we ensure adherence to your approved terminology and style and the proper display of formatted content;
  5. afterwards, we perform a keen review of the translated content and coordinate with you for further instructions or stage approvals; by the way, we constantly refer to the assumed quality standard;
  6. we subsequently make our preliminary delivery and await for feedback, if needed, on any additional changes to further implement in your project;
  7. upon our final delivery, we file the project information for future reference and use.
